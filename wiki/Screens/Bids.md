<h1>Bid Screens</h1>
<p>
    Within the application there are two screens for directly working with Bids. The Bid Maintenance Screen &amp; the Bid Edit Dialog. This documentation will
    help the user to understand how to use those interfaces &amp; understand their avaialable functions.
</p>

<h2>Bid Maintenance Screen</h2>
<p>
    The Bid Maintenance Screen is used to view all the available bids, edit them, delete them, or create new bids. It also has available actions to do operations directly
    upon the bids. As well as reporting. This screen is special within the application as it's the only screen to include the "Config" button in the top right.
</p>
<br />

<h3>Available Operations</h3>
<p>
    Below are common operations for working with these screens.
</p>
<div class="card">
    <h5 class="card-header">How do I create a new Bid?</h5>
    <div class="card-body">
        <p>
            You are able to create a new bid at any time. To do this go to the main Bid Maintenance screen, at
            the top left select the Add button. Alternatively you can press the "Insert" or "A" keys on your keyboard.
            You will now be prompted with a window to choose the bid name.
        </p>

        <h6>Click Add Buton</h6>
        <p>
            Note the bid name cannot be blank, it must be unique, & it must have a length of less than 255 characters.
            After entering a bid name, you can now click save & the bid will be created.
        </p>
    </div>
</div>
<br />

<div class="card">
    <h5 class="card-header">How do I edit the name of a Bid?</h5>
    <div class="card-body">
        <p>
            You are able to edit the name of a bid at any time. To do this go to the main Bid Maintenance screen,
            at the top left select the Edit button. Alternatively you can press the "E" key on your keyboard.
            You will now be prompted with a window to edit the bid name.
        </p>

        <h6>Click Edit Button</h6>
        <p>
            Note the bid name cannot be blank, it must be unique, & it must have a length of less than 255 characters.
            After entering a bid name, you can now click save & the bid will be updated.
        </p>
    </div>
</div>
<br />

<div class="card">
    <h5 class="card-header">How do I delete a Bid?</h5>
    <div class="card-body">
        <p>
            You are able to delete a bid at any time. To do this, you must have selected to allow bid deletion
            under configuration (see How do I gain access to deleting bids?), afterwards go to the main Bid
            Maintenance screen, at the top left select the Delete button. Alternatively you can press the "D"
            key on your keyboard. You will now be prompted to confirm your choice.
        </p>

        <h6>Click Delete Button</h6>
        <p>
            Note that this operation cannot be undone. It is recommended to export bid fully before deleting it.

            After deleting the bid, the abillity to delete bids will be set to default OFF state.
        </p>
    </div>
</div>
<br />
